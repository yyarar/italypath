# ItalyPath · 12s vertical production breakdown

## Style block

Use ItalyPath’s real editorial system: `#f8f7f1` paper, `#15201c` ink,
`#1f4f46` sage, `#b75b38` terracotta, and a restrained `#6f2638` burgundy
supporting accent. Headlines use Spectral 600/700; utility details use Hanken
Grotesk. The visual world is warm Italian editorial cinema with tactile film
grain, deep olive shadows, and quiet morning light.

Concept angle: ItalyPath turns an aspiration into a sequence of tangible
thresholds — a street, a plan on the table, a moving train, then a calm wordmark.

Rhythm: `dream — plan — momentum — arrival`.

## Global rules

- 1080×1920, 30fps, 12 seconds.
- Essential copy stays inside x 88–992 and y 250–1580.
- Keep all text as HTML/video layers. Photography contains no text.
- Use a slow Ken Burns move in every photographic scene.
- Use the same gentle 0.6s cross-dissolve at 4s, 8s, and 10s.
- Background: full-bleed photography plus subtle grade, scrim, vignette, and
  film-grain layers.
- Midground: one short Spectral headline per beat.
- Foreground: hairline rule, scene numeral, restrained registration corners,
  and a small ItalyPath identifier.

## Scene 01 · 0.0–4.0s

Concept: A calm Roman street at sunrise is the dream before it has become a
task list. The empty cobbles and warm façades should feel possible, intimate,
and lived-in.

Depth:

- BG: dawn street photo, warm grade, edge vignette, fine grain.
- MG: “İtalya hayalin” in cream Spectral.
- FG: terracotta rule, `01` numeral, two registration corners, small
  `ITALYPATH` identifier.

Choreography: photo drifts forward and slightly right; the rule grows
horizontally; title rises 26px and resolves from soft opacity; utility details
fade in at different, restrained offsets.

Transition: 0.6s cross-dissolve centered around 4.0s.

## Scene 02 · 4.0–8.0s

Concept: The dream becomes tactile and actionable. Espresso, a plain travel
booklet, and an unlabeled outline map of Italy sit in real morning light.

Depth:

- BG: tabletop photo, warm grade, directional shadow, fine grain.
- MG: “bir plana dönüşsün.” in cream Spectral.
- FG: terracotta rule, `02` numeral, registration corners, small
  `ITALYPATH` identifier.

Choreography: image eases outward from the map toward the cup; title enters
with a gentle upward drift after the dissolve begins; foreground details settle
quietly around it.

Transition: 0.6s cross-dissolve centered around 8.0s.

## Scene 03 · 8.0–10.0s

Concept: Momentum replaces planning. A train window frames the Italian
landscape and a burgundy bag suggests the new chapter is already underway.

Depth:

- BG: train photo, olive shadow grade, subtle moving-window light, fine grain.
- MG: “Doğru adımlarla.” in cream Spectral.
- FG: terracotta rule, `03` numeral, registration corners, small
  `ITALYPATH` identifier.

Choreography: image pans very slightly with the direction of travel; headline
appears promptly and holds; the rule lands last to punctuate the statement.

Transition: 0.6s cross-dissolve centered around 10.0s.

## Scene 04 · 10.0–12.0s

Concept: A minimal editorial close. The journey resolves into the ItalyPath
name as the calm, trustworthy guide.

Depth:

- BG: solid sage field, localized soft cream glow, subtle grain.
- MG: “ItalyPath” in cream Spectral 700.
- FG: terracotta rule, `04` numeral, minimal registration corners.

Choreography: wordmark fades and rises into place; the terracotta rule draws;
the card holds long enough to read and then gently settles at 12.0s.

## Negative prompt

No postcard clichés, no flags, no landmarks collage, no neon, no oversaturated
orange/teal grade, no text baked into images, no fast zooms, no bounce easing,
no crowded lower-third card, no UI-like rounded rectangles, and no essential
content in Instagram’s top or bottom interface zones.
