# Image generation prompts

All three production images were generated with the built-in `imagegen` path
as separate `photorealistic-natural` assets. Shared constraints: exact vertical
9:16 intent, premium editorial travel photography, restrained 35mm texture,
warm cream/terracotta highlights, deep olive shadows, subtle burgundy accents,
usable negative space for later video typography, and no text, logo, watermark,
caption, or border.

## Scene 01

Calm, authentic Roman residential street at sunrise; weathered ochre and pale
stone façades, shutters, cobblestones, a distant vintage Italian scooter, no
crowds, soft golden dawn, generous lower-middle copy space, no postcard
landmarks.

## Scene 02

Real dark-walnut café table beside a softly lit window; ceramic espresso,
plain worn burgundy travel booklet, cream folded paper with a simple unlabeled
outline of Italy; no emblem, lettering, labels, roads, symbols, numbers, or
typographic marks anywhere.

## Scene 03

View from inside a modern Italian regional train; large window looking onto
rolling central-Italian hills, a distant hill town and cypress trees in morning
light; empty window seat and burgundy travel bag; subtle natural motion outside,
no people, signage, tickets, or logos.
