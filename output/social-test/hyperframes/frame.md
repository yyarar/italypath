# ItalyPath · Instagram Story Test

## Format

- Canvas: 1080 × 1920 portrait
- Duration: 12 seconds
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Destination: Instagram Story / Reel
- Safe area: keep essential copy inside x 88–992 and y 250–1580

## Brand truth

- Editorial paper: `#f8f7f1`
- Editorial ink: `#15201c`
- Editorial sage: `#1f4f46`
- Terracotta: `#b75b38`
- Supporting burgundy: `#6f2638`
- Headline: Spectral, 600–700
- Utility copy: Hanken Grotesk, 500–700

## Mood

Warm Italian editorial cinema: calm, tactile, aspirational, and grounded. The
piece should feel like a thoughtful first step rather than a tourist advert.

## Composition

- Full-bleed photography with restrained warm/olive grading.
- Cream headline text over a controlled lower-middle scrim.
- Hairline rules, restrained frame marks, scene numerals, and subtle film grain
  provide foreground detail without competing with the message.
- Main headline width: approximately 70–78% of the frame.
- All typography is a video layer; generated photography contains no text.

## Motion

- Natural Ken Burns movement: 1.03–1.08 scale across each scene with a small,
  directional translation.
- Gentle 0.6-second cross-dissolves centered on the 4s, 8s, and 10s beats.
- Text entrances use a quiet upward drift and opacity reveal.
- No aggressive zooms, bounces, spins, glitches, or high-energy transitions.

## Do not

- Do not use gradients as headline fills.
- Do not use pure white or pure black.
- Do not put essential copy in the top 250px or bottom 340px.
- Do not add a graphic logo; the existing brand uses an editorial ItalyPath
  wordmark.
